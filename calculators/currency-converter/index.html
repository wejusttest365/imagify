Live Rate vs. Bank Exchange Rate

Why the rate you see here can differ from what your bank offers

Mid-Market Rate
  • The real, midpoint exchange rate quoted on global currency markets.
  • Used by this converter so you can see the true value of your money.
  • Best as a benchmark before comparing offers from banks or transfer services.
Bank / Card Rate
  • Banks and card networks typically add a margin on top of the mid-market rate.
  • The amount you actually receive or are charged may be slightly less favorable.
  • Always compare your provider's quoted rate against the live rate shown here.

This tool displays the live mid-market exchange rate only. It does not include transaction fees, card markups or transfer charges that a bank or provider may apply.
