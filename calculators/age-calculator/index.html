How to Use the Age Calculator

Get your exact age in just 3 simple steps

1
Enter Your Date of Birth

Click the date field and select your birth date from the calendar picker. You can also type it directly in the format shown.

2
Set the Calculation Date (Optional)

By default, your age is calculated up to today. You can optionally choose a specific date — great for calculating age on a past or future date.

3
Click Calculate Age

Hit the Calculate button and instantly see your age in years, months, weeks, days, and total hours — plus how many days until your next birthday.
