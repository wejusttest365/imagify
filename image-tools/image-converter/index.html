    Frequently Asked Questions About Image Format Conversion

    Everything you need to know about converting images between JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF and other formats online.

    What is an online image converter?

    An online image converter is a browser-based tool that lets you change an image from one file format to another — for example, converting a JPG photo to PNG, or a PNG logo to WEBP, or a GIF to JPG — without installing any software or paying for expensive apps like Photoshop. Our tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API, meaning no files are ever uploaded to an external server.

    Is this image converter completely free?

    Yes, this image converter is 100% completely free to use with no hidden charges, no premium plans, no file limits per day, and no credit card required. We keep it free for everyone forever.

    Are my images uploaded to a server?

    No. All image conversions happen entirely inside your browser using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never sent to or stored on any external server or cloud service. This makes the tool completely private and secure — your files stay on your device at all times.

    Can I convert multiple images at once?

    Yes. Our image converter supports full batch conversion — you can upload and convert multiple image files simultaneously. After conversion, you can download all your converted files individually or together as a single ZIP archive for convenience.

    Will converting an image reduce its quality?

    It depends on the conversion type and formats involved. Converting to PNG or WEBP preserves quality very well since these are lossless or near-lossless formats. Converting to JPG applies lossy compression which slightly reduces quality but produces much smaller file sizes. For most uses — websites, documents, email, sharing — the quality difference is not noticeable to the human eye.

    Why should I convert JPG to PNG?

    Convert JPG to PNG when you need: (1) Transparency support for logos or graphics with transparent backgrounds, (2) Lossless quality preservation — PNG does not degrade when re-saved or re-edited, and (3) Sharp edges on text, icons, or diagrams. JPG is better for photographs where smaller file size matters more than transparency. Use this converter to easily switch from JPG's lossy compression to PNG's lossless preservation.

    Why should I convert images to WEBP?

    WEBP is a modern image format developed by Google that delivers significantly smaller file sizes than both JPG and PNG — often 25–35% smaller — with the same or better visual quality. This means faster page load times, better Core Web Vitals scores, improved SEO rankings in Google Search, and reduced bandwidth costs. All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers support WEBP natively.

    What file types does this converter support?

    Currently this tool supports conversion between: JPG (JPEG), PNG, WEBP, and GIF formats. Supported conversion pairs include: JPG→PNG, PNG→JPG, WEBP→PNG, WEBP→JPG, PNG→WEBP, JPG→WEBP, GIF→JPG, and more. The tool automatically detects the input format and shows compatible output options. Only files matching the selected conversion pair will be accepted during upload.

    Does this tool work on mobile?

    Yes. The image converter is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets running iOS (iPhone, iPad) or Android. You can upload images directly from your phone's camera roll, photo library, or files app and download converted images straight to your device storage.
