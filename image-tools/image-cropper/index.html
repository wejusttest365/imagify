FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Cropping Images

Everything you need to know about cropping images online quickly and safely.

How do I crop an image online for free?

Upload your image, drag the crop box to select the area you want to keep or resize it from any corner handle, optionally lock it to a ratio like Square or 16:9, then click Crop and download the result.

Can I crop an image to a specific ratio like 1:1 or 16:9?

Yes, the tool includes one-tap presets for Free, Square, 4:3, 16:9 and 3:2 ratios, and the crop box automatically snaps to your chosen ratio while you drag.

Does cropping reduce image quality?

No. Cropping only removes pixels outside the selected area; the remaining pixels keep their original quality since no scaling or compression is applied unless you separately choose to resize.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere when I crop them?

No, cropping is done entirely in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never sent to a server, keeping them completely private.

What image formats can I crop?

You can crop JPG, PNG, WEBP and GIF images, and the cropped result downloads in the same format as your original file.

Can I undo my crop and start over?

Yes, click Reset at any time to restore the crop box to its default position and size and start selecting again without re-uploading the image.
