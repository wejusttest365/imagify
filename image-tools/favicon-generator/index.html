Why Use a Favicon Generator?




A favicon generator helps you create professional website icons in multiple sizes for browsers, bookmarks, mobile devices, and app shortcuts. Instead of manually designing each icon size, this tool automatically generates all required favicon files in seconds.





Favicons improve your website branding and make your site look more trustworthy. When users open multiple tabs, a favicon helps them quickly recognize your website.





This online favicon generator is simple to use. Upload your logo or image, customize padding and background color, then download a complete favicon pack including PNG icons, favicon.ico, and manifest.json.
