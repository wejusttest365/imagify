FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Resizing Images

Everything you need to know about resizing images online quickly and safely.

How do I resize an image online for free?

Upload your image into the tool above, enter your target width and height in pixels (or switch to percentage), keep the lock icon on to preserve the aspect ratio, then click Resize and download the result. Everything runs in your browser and takes only a few seconds.

Will resizing reduce my image quality?

Shrinking an image (downscaling) keeps quality very close to the original. Enlarging an image (upscaling) beyond its original resolution can introduce softness since the tool has to estimate new pixels that weren't in the source file.

What's the difference between resizing by pixels and by percentage?

Resizing by pixels sets an exact width and height, useful when a platform requires a specific size like 1200×630. Resizing by percentage scales the whole image proportionally, useful when you just want it smaller or larger without hitting an exact number.

Does this tool upload my images to a server?

No. All resizing happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your device, so the tool is private and secure by design.

Can I resize JPG, PNG, WEBP and GIF images?

Yes, the resizer accepts JPG, PNG, WEBP and GIF files and lets you download the resized output in the same format as your original file.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is needed. The resizer works entirely inside your web browser on desktop, tablet or mobile — just open the page and start resizing.
