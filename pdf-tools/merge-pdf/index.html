Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Merge PDF Tool

1. How do I merge PDF files online?

Upload your PDF files, arrange them in order, and click the “Merge PDF” button. Your combined file will be ready for download instantly.

2. Is this PDF merge tool free to use?

Yes, WebToolOcean PDF Merge tool is completely free. No signup or subscription is required.

3. Is it safe to upload my files?

Yes, your files are processed securely in the browser and are not stored on our servers.

4. Can I merge more than two PDF files?

Yes, you can merge multiple PDF files at once and rearrange their order before merging.

5. Will the quality of my PDF change after merging?

No, the tool preserves original quality, formatting, and resolution of all PDF files.

6. Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required. The tool works directly in your browser.
