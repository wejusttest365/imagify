Best Way to Compress Images

Struggling with 'File Too Large' errors when emailing PDFs? Our compression tool reduces size while keeping your text and images sharp.

Why Use This Tool?

Large PDFs take up storage and are difficult to upload. Compressing them makes your website faster and your emails more reliable.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload your heavy PDF document.
  2. Choose the level of compression (Basic or High).
  3. Wait a few seconds for our algorithm to optimize the file.
  4. Download your lightened PDF.
Pro Tip: Basic compression maintains high print quality, while High compression is best for web/screen viewing.

Key Benefits

  • Significant size reduction (up to 90%).
  • Optimized for web and email.
  • Zero visible quality loss for text.
