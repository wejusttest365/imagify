Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Imagify Resume (“we”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our resume builder at https://wejusttest365.github.io/imagify/resume/.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Data Entered by Users

All resume information you type (name, job title, skills, etc.) is stored only in your browser. We do not store, upload, or transmit your resume data to our servers.

b. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect non-personal data such as:

  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Cookies required for Google Ads or Analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To run and improve the resume builder
  • To show relevant Google Ads (per AdSense policy)
  • To analyze traffic and prevent abuse

3. Google Ads & Cookies

We use Google AdSense. Google may use cookies such as:

  • Ad personalization cookies
  • Analytics cookies
  • Non-personal user identifiers

You can opt out of ad personalization anytime here:
https://www.google.com/settings/ads

4. Data Storage

We do not store your resume data. All content stays locally in your browser until you download it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Third parties like Google may collect non-personal data for analytics and ads.

6. Your Rights

  • You can clear site data anytime from your browser.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads.
  • You can delete downloaded resumes from your device at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

This service is not designed for children under 13.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always appear on this page.
