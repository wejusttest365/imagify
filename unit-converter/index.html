Metric vs. Imperial Units

Both systems are supported — here's how they differ in scope

Imperial / US Units
  • Includes inches, feet, miles, pounds, ounces, gallons and Fahrenheit.
  • Commonly used in the United States for everyday measurements.
  • Often based on historical, non-decimal unit relationships.
Metric (SI) Units
  • Includes meters, kilograms, liters, Celsius and Kelvin.
  • The standard system used by most countries and in science globally.
  • Based on powers of ten, making conversions within the system simple.

This tool converts seamlessly between both systems within each category, so you never need to manually look up a conversion factor.
